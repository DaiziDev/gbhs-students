About

Goverment Bilingual High School Koutaba

GBHS-KOUTABA is a vibrant and dynamic school dedicated to fostering academic excellence and personal growth. The school caters to a wide range of academic interests,

It uniquely offers both a francophone session, accommodating students from 6ème to Terminale, and an anglophone session from Form 1 to Upper Sixth, providing a bilingual environment that promotes cultural diversity and linguistic proficiency.

Including a both arts and sciences course, ensuring students receive a well-rounded education. Facilities at GBHS-KOUTABA are top-notch, featuring a modern laboratory equipped for scientific experiments and research, as well as a well-equipped computer lab that supports digital learning and technological skills.

The school also boasts expansive sports facilities, including a large football and handball field, encouraging students to stay active, develop teamwork, and enjoy a healthy lifestyle. Overall, GBHS-KOUTABA is a nurturing educational community dedicated to shaping the future leaders of tomorrow through quality education and comprehensive extracurricular activities.
